At Sweet Chai & Co., we believe in bringing people together over the warmth of chai and the joy of desserts. Rooted in Pakistani tradition and inspired by modern flavors, our café offers a halal-friendly menu of handcrafted drinks, indulgent sweets, and cozy vibes. Whether you're here for your favorite lassi, a quick mithai fix, or just a quiet place to unwind — you’re always welcome at Sweet Chai & Co.
